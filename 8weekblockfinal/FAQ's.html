Frequently Asked Questions

Your question might already have been answered! Below are some of our more common ones.



What is a nature tour?

A nature tour is an outdoor activity where you explore scenic landscapes, wildlife, and ecosystems with the guidance of an expert tour guide.

How long are the tours?

Our tours vary in length depending on the trail or activity, ranging from a few hours to full-day adventures.

Do I need experience to join a tour?

No experience is required! We offer tours for beginners, intermediate, and advanced hikers. Our guides will tailor the experience to your skill level.

What should I bring on the tour?

We recommend bringing water, sunscreen, appropriate footwear, and a camera to capture the beautiful scenery!
